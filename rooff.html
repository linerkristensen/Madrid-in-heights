THE VINCCI MINT

This hotel is located in the heart of Gran Vía in Madrid is the Vincci The Mint 4* hotel. The hotel offers guests an innovative and cutting-edge concept created by the famous interior designer Jaime Beriestain who, while respecting the essence of the building, bestows it with his own particular style. On this rooftop, the hotel has an exclusive terrace with stunning views of the Gran Vía and where you can enjoy a snack al fresco in true American style from the foodtruck.
