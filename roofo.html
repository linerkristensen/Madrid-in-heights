EL PARACAIDISTA

On the low level is the art gallery and the movie-bar where they show thematic screenings. The first and second floor are the shopping areas with really cool, unique and original products, perfect for a gift!

On the third floor is the Cuban cocktail bar inspired by the old clubs of La Habana. On the fourth and last floor is “Parq” the terrace restaurant with amazing views of the roofs of Malasaña (both bars close at 00:00).
